Welcome to Part 3: Optinal Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will decide whether to delegate all allocation decisions in this part to an AI agent or make all decisions yourself. This choice must be made before any round begins and applies to all 10 rounds in this part. Once you make your decision, it cannot be changed.

How This Works

  1. Decide Upfront: At the start of this part, you will choose whether to:
    • Not delegate and make every allocation decision yourself for all 10 rounds, similarly to what you did in Part 1.
    • Delegate all allocation decisions for all 10 rounds to an AI agent and set up this agent just like you did in Part 2.
  2. Reshuffled receivers: Once again, you will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  3. Irreversible Choice: Your decision applies to all rounds in this part. If you choose to delegate, the AI agent will make all decisions. If you choose not to delegate, you will make every decision yourself.

Welcome to Part 2: No Delegation

In this part of the experiment, you will make allocation decisions yourself for each of the 10 rounds. Unlike other parts, there is no delegation. You will decide how to allocate the bonuses directly between yourself and the other participant in each round.

How This Works

  1. Decide Your Allocation: In each round, you will write how much of the 100 Ecoins you would like to allocate to the other participant (the Receiver) matched with you in that round.
  2. Reshuffled receivers: You will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  3. You keep the rest:The remainder of the 100 ECoins will be yours to keep.
  4. Once you are satisfied with your decision: Click "Confirm" to finalize your choice for that round. Your decisions are final once submitted and cannot be changed.
